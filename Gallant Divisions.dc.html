Circular economy

Gallant Loop Africa

Every event leaves materials behind. We put them back to work.

Events are resource-heavy. Gallant Loop Africa is our circular economy division, built to change that, upcycling post-use banners, lanyards, stage fabrics and signage into new delegate kits and corporate products.

For clients and donors with ESG targets, GLA turns disposal cost into a measurable, reportable advantage.
