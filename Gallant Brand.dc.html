Using the mark

Do
  • ✓Keep clear space around the mark equal to the height of the lion.
  • ✓Use the reversed white mark on ink or photography.
  • ✓Reproduce the lion no smaller than 24px tall on screen.
Don't
  • ✕Stretch, rotate, recolour or add effects to the mark.
  • ✕Place the ink mark on busy or low-contrast backgrounds.
  • ✕Wash the accent gradient across backgrounds or headlines.
